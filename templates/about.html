About PurrMetrics 🐱

Cats instinctively hide signs of pain or discomfort, making it difficult for owners to notice when something is wrong. PurrMetrics uses sensors and data analysis to monitor litter habits, food intake, water consumption and movement behaviour, giving owners early insights into their cat’s health.


React Frontend Flask API SQLite DB IoT Sensors

The Problem

Cats often hide illness. By the time symptoms appear, the condition may already be serious. Owners have limited ways to track subtle behaviour changes in their pets.

The Solution

PurrMetrics collects behavioural data from sensors attached to litter boxes, food bowls and hiding spots. The system detects changes in behaviour and displays them through a dashboard.

Why We Track

Food intake, water consumption, litter box usage and hiding behaviour can reveal early signs of health issues such as kidney disease, infections or stress.

The Benefit

By analysing behaviour patterns over time, PurrMetrics can help owners identify unusual trends and seek veterinary care earlier.
