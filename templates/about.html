About PurrMetrics 🐱

PurrMetrics is a smart feline monitoring system designed to detect early signs of illness through behavioural tracking. Using sensors and data analysis, it monitors litter usage, food intake, water consumption, and hiding behaviour.


Flask SQLite IoT Sensors Analytics

🐾 The Problem

Cats hide illness extremely well. By the time symptoms appear, it may already be serious.

💡 The Solution

We track subtle behaviour changes using sensors and visualise them through an intuitive dashboard.

📊 What We Track

• Litter box usage
• Food intake
• Water consumption
• Hiding behaviour

🚀 The Benefit

Early detection of abnormal patterns helps owners act quickly and improve their cat’s health outcomes.
